# ATS Keyword Extractor Prompt

## Goal

Extract important ATS keywords from a job description.

## Instructions

Given a job description, extract:

- Technical skills
- Soft skills
- Tools and technologies
- Required qualifications
- Industry terms
- Important keywords

Group the keywords into categories.
